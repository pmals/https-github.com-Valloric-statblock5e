Legendary Resistance (3/Day)

Anti-Magic Brand

Any time Yagdkobs is targeted by a spell, roll a d6. On a 1 to 5, Yagdkobs is unaffected. On a 6, Yagdkobs is unaffected, and the effect is reflected back at the caster as though it originated from Yagdkobs, turning the caster into the target.
